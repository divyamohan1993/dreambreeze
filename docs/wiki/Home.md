# DreamBreeze Wiki

Welcome to the DreamBreeze knowledge base -- the AI-powered sleep comfort system that turns your phone into an intelligent sleep companion.

## What is DreamBreeze?

DreamBreeze uses your phone's accelerometer to detect sleep posture in real time, then controls fan speed, soundscapes, and wake timing through a four-agent AI system -- all running on-device with zero cloud dependency.

**No wearable. No special hardware. No subscription.**
